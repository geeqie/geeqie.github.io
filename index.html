Fast and accurate

Fast preview for many raw image formats and many other supported.

Color management. Histogram and Pixel information

Split View (up to 4) for image comparison

Organize without losing space

History

Geeqie is the successor to GqView, which was first
released in 1998.

In 2007 GqView was renamed Geeqie.

Over the years many new features have been implemented.

Since the start of the project more than 3000 commits
have been made, and the project continues to be actively maintained.
